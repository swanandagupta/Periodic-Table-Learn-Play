HOW TO PLAY!

  • -> Begin by launching the game to view the interactive periodic table.
  • Click on any element from the periodic table to start the quiz associated with that element.
  • Once you select an element, a quiz with questions related to that element will appear. Read each question and choose the correct answer from the provided options.
  • After answering all the questions for the selected element, submit your responses to receive a score specifically for that element.
  • -> For noble gases you can play tiles dodging game which relates to noble gases being unreactive
  • when the maximum number of electron in outer orbital is touched the game stops and you win the round
  • -> For La and Ac series we collect extra electons by electron collecting game until we collect the maximum number of electrons then u win the round
  • -> Replay the game to improve your scores, challenge yourself with different elements, and enhance your understanding of the periodic table.
  • -> Enjoy the process as you learn about each element and test your knowledge through interactive quizzes!
